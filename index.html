System Overview

One closed-loop system, from market mapping to pipeline.

CDS is a fully integrated sourcing infrastructure built for private equity funds and operating platforms. It maps entire markets at scale, structures all relevant acquisition targets, enriches owner and operator data, automates prioritization and outreach, and tracks pipeline creation across every portfolio company in one place.

LAYER 01

Pipeline & Project Command Center

Sourcing organized into structured pipelines for each portfolio company, market thesis, or geographic initiative. Centralized visibility, task tracking, and unified search across every project.

LAYER 02

Data Ingestion & Market Mapping

Large-scale scraping of company, website, and directory data. Deduplication and normalization at scale. Geographic clustering to surface density, whitespace, and full-market coverage.

LAYER 03

Structured Target Database

Every company consolidated into a clean, proprietary dataset scaling from 10k to 100k+ targets. Standardized fields, advanced filtering, and segmentation owned entirely by the fund.

LAYER 04

Data Transformation & Intelligence

Website parsing, classification (multi-location, independent, franchise), tagging, and scoring logic. Moves sourcing from raw data to a ranked, prioritized opportunity set.

LAYER 05

Contact Enrichment

Owner and operator identification, LinkedIn profile matching, work email and phone enrichment. Every target tied to outreach-ready decision-makers with status tracking end to end.

LAYER 06

Automation (Watcher System)

Rule-based triggers that continuously monitor the dataset, assign leads, route opportunities, and schedule campaigns. Always-on activation that is system-driven, not rep-dependent.

LAYER 07

Outreach & Campaign Execution

Integrated outbound execution with campaign sequencing, email platform integration, performance tracking, and pipeline attribution. Data, action, and pipeline creation in one system.

LAYER 08

Analytics & Performance Tracking

Real-time visibility into lead counts, enrichment rates, outreach activity, and geographic performance. Benchmarking across markets and pipelines enables data-driven optimization.

The Closed Loop

Every cycle compounds the fund's proprietary intelligence.

Every interaction feeds the system. Targets identified, contacts enriched, emails sent, and responses tracked all return to the dataset, continuously improving targeting, prioritization, and conversion across the entire portfolio.
